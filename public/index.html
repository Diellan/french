Welcome!

This experiment is intended for individuals of age 18 to 35, who were exposed only to English at the age of six, and who do not have any familiarity with Hebrew, Arabic, or other Semitic languages (or derivatives like Yiddish or Ladino).

Individuals with learning or cognitive disabilities (such as dyslexia or strong ADHD), or visual impairments that are not correctable (corrected with glasses is fine), should also be excluded (sorry!).

This experiment should be performed in a lab-like setting (aka somewhere quiet and free of distractions, especially audible words).

It will take roughly 10 minutes to perform.

Questions can be directed to the reddit thread or to citizengenia@gmail.com.
